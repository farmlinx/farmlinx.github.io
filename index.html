Celebrate the farmer!

Organic supply chain is fraught with issues relating to source traceability, transparency, accountability and credibility. Farmlinx bridges these gaps by connecting organic producers to buyers in a verifiable and trustworthy manner. Farmlinx empowers organic farmers establish a certified reputation.

Why Blockchain?

Farmlinx is powered by Qubento's blockchain technology 

Blockchain technology enables end-to-end traceability of products, by bringing together all relevant stakeholders in the organic supply chain

The distributed ledger technology results in database ledgers whose entries are immutable, thereby providing a reliable record of the transaction history

The permissioned blockchain network makes sure that no rogue actors find their way into the supply chain, thereby enabling the integrity of the transactions

Electronically written ‘smart contracts’ between the relevant stakeholders ensure that the trading is carried out smoothly
